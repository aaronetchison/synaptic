About Us

Synaptic Media was established in 2005 by Jacob Phillips as a Web developing and marketing company, and has rapidly become an industry leader.

We have expanded our business several times in recent years to turn Synaptic Media into a complete media production company to provide its customers with products that fulfill all of your media needs.

Our team consists of a variety of full-time and freelance talent that includes Web developers, application developers, graphic designers, animators, market researchers, video producers and audio engineers.

Our client list contains small businesses and several Fortune 500 companies, such as Intel and Nike.

If you are looking for an innovative and reliable company to fulfill all of your media needs, then Synaptic Media is the right choice.
